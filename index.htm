About Me     My Publications     Software     Links

 

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Medical School of Michigan University advised by Dr. Jieping Ye. I worked in the School of Computer Science at Carnegie Mellon University from July 2014 to Sep 2015 advised by Dr. Alexander G. Hauptmann. I received my Ph.D. degree in computer science from Tsinghua University in 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Chuangshui Zhang. During my Ph.D. study, I had been a visiting scholar in Michigan State University advised by Prof. Rong Jin from Nov 2012 to Dec 2013 and in CMU from Dec 2013 to July 2014. My research interest mainly focuses on high dimensional high order statistics and the related applications in real world machine learning problems.
